04.08.2022

In the midst of chaos and loud screeching
Dancing to the tunes of scorching waves
Silence sits on a green throne weaved of sunbeams through cracked caves

As if in a moment of limbo
Between the last drop of a creek
And the gurgle of ocean at time bleak

Sits in meditation and prayer
A mystic with no questions nor wisdom
Surrendered to a failed language only the lonely revere

A temple for small creatures
A shrine for fallen features
A plane uneven softly reveals

Redeeming demeaning whispers
Seeming endearing yet secretly hinder
A story so holy of tears and wrinkles

No rhythm nor rhyme
No truth nor secrets
The sounds of shivers from deep down under

The mystic, in a state of paralysis
Observes crackling bark
Absorbs a dampening singing lark
Captive of a daze so stark
And on a journey to embark

Few bells will chime
Less urgency with time
For all else was frozen
In the presence of the one chosen
