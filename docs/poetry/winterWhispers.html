25.10.2022

Time is lighter
Breath is weighted
The sun stands with rays that donâ€™t pierce but fiercely dive
Through clouds that are shy of dipping into ice-cold water
Tasting the colours of heavenly brushes that have often softly rested between the curves of mountains and branches of trees gracefully reaching in hopes to kiss the space between dreams and drunken truths

Trails commemorate lost stories of denial and lead reckless paths of adventure
Snow dust dampens questions of love and God and existence and the time between waking and dreaming and the instant between seizing and ceasing and the light between a whisper and a heart and the gaze between a child and a mother

I stand drowned by a silence so refreshing
Held by an unreal and overwhelming power in the form of a peak
A peak which sees over descendants
Allows very little dependence
Confronts in subtle remembrance
Bodies of water sunken in dutiful repentance

All around as the sight wanders
Glory in the form of mountains stretches like a metaphor for the women who wether gusts of living and shape-shift into soaring beings with diamonds for hearts and magic for minds
Who rest on the palms of their hands travellers as they seek home and comfort
And who hold within the crevices of their wisdom songs of the past and hymns of the present
And who tell in tender tongues tales about the times when the warmth of a day ran waterfalls through their veins and floated the rivers under their feet
