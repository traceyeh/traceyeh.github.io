09.04.2022

The wind caresses the trees as they hum softly and playfully like a gathering of children trying to be quiet as the grown ups debate
It also gently rubs on the very surface of the water blowing onto it shapes and ripples meticulously lining the meeting points of two elements
I sit and listen and watch
Wondering what the geese are fussing about
Trying to let go of my fragile ego
Entwined with emotions to which I am not sure I'm entitled
Creating scenarios where like the wind, I softly blow on the surface of the heart
Cautiously lining the point where reason and feelings meet
Outlining a story with two narrators
Entangled as they untangle a web of premeditated
Complex and made so by intoxicated movements of the body and the eyes and the lips
And words empty as words in stories are
Making ways into your self as you understand your relationship to them
But the art is the lack of meaning and connection to you and the characters and even the story
Isn’t it a craft of passing moments
Designed with malice to paint
A pseudo world where nothing actually happens?

But to close the book before the author decided you should
Is to betray the potential of a fleeting beating of a pulse not intended for any purpose
A betrayal of the narrator who didn’t wrap the twist
And the page that didn’t just yet bleed its ink

So we read and pause
Close our eyes and get close
To every movement of a body and eyes and lips
As we see it through our own

We hear voices we create

And at that point
Realizing we made our own
Plot and twist and knot and are now entangled with the narrators
One in fiction and made up friction between ourselves and the one whose self is far from the page
Separated from the story
There to make you spin and in your heart put a pin

You never forget the character whose words slide off the page
Like ripples on wind-blown waters
Going nowhere
Dissipating as they come to life

Though there lies the beauty of the spoken
Its mystery, mere history, and an effect tragic and eternal
For all there is to words
Is their natural ephemeral
