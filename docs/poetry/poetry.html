Finding Patterns Half the fun is in the searching

Poetry has mostly been a private venue for processing thoughts, questions, and at time feelings. I've resorted to writing as a means to keep in touch with the urges that have found no better outlet than chaotic and unstructured words. I dabble in different approaches, and find the outcome to often steer away from rhyme. However, Rhythm remains present.
